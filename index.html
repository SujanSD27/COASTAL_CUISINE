Welcome to CoastalCuisine,

where we blend culinary excellence with warm hospitality. Our journey started with a dream to craft unforgettable dining experiences. At CoastalCuisine, we celebrate tradition while embracing innovation with our diverse menu. Join us for a cozy, inviting atmosphere that enhances every dining occasion. Thank you for choosing CoastalCuisine – where flavors meet smiles!

  • Passion for Culinary Excellence: At CoastalCuisine, we are driven by an unwavering passion for culinary perfection. Our chefs combine creativity and tradition to create dishes that tantalize your taste buds.
  • Warm and Inviting Ambiance: Step into our cozy restaurant, where the welcoming aroma of our creations sets the stage for an unforgettable dining experience. Whether it's an intimate dinner or a special celebration, our ambiance enhances every moment.
  • Community Commitment: We are more than just a restaurant; we are a part of the Bangalore community. We support local initiatives, use fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, and strive for sustainability in every dish we serve. Join us in savoring the art of food and hospitality.
